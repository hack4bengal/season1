Using the internet to be together

Though the Hackathon is offline this time, we will be virtually connected with our community online!

  • Live Stream of Events online, via Youtube. Events if held offline will be streamed online.
  • Giveaways, Prizes and Surprises for every event will consider both online and offline participation.
  • Though the Hackathon will be in Kolkata, events might take place in different cities. So every community member might get a chance to attend a live event.

Every contribution will be rewarded

Whatever might be the contribution, there will be swags for everyone! If you think its a clickbait, you are wrong. A whole new experience awaits.

  • When we say swags for all, we mean it!
  • Exclusive Goodies which might include pens, stickers, t-shirts and who knows what
  • Every successful submission will be rewarded, this is to ensure the community gets motivation to participate

After Season 1, Season 2 Awaits

  • With the grand ending of H4B 1.0, the wait for 2.0 began.
  • A new team, with new skills await you at Hack4bengal. Doubel swags and more awards await you.
  • Karenge nai, Karna hi parega is the motto we believe is the communities key to success, let the Hack begin.

Want to join our team? Get in touch with the Organisers if you want to volunteer for Hack4Bengal. Your ideas and our vision, we will make and build for Bengal. Hackathons are where crazy ideas become a reality.

Building Communities, one at a time

What started as a team of 3, is now a community of 1500+ Hackers and still counting. We believe in the fact that, whenever we give time to the community, it always gives back to us.

  • Community Partners: 31+ and still counting, including GDG Kolkata and GDG Cloud Kolkata
  • Individual Events hosted by H4B, both offline and online is near about: 85
  • 35+ Sponsors who support our team and believe in our cause of giving back to the community!

Hackathon Timeline

The Hackathon will not only be a 36 Hours blast, it would be a timeline of madness. Stay tuned with us for an awesome experience.

Registrations Start

Start registering for Hack4Bengal with your teammates and reach out to us if you have any queries.

Registration Closes

Registrations close and member screening will be done at this phase. Eligible (Anyone completing the entire regn process) Teams/ Participants will go on to the next phase.

Pre Hacks and Live Hacks

Various community events to be held online and offline for a number of days before the hackathon officially kicks off.

Winners Announce with Closing Ceremony

Announcement of winning teams along with prize distribution and closing ceremony will be done, with ofc H4B 3.0 Announcement.
